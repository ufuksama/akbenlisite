Our Heritage

Founded in 1998, Akbenli emerged from a vision to establish Turkey's first specialized horseshoe nail manufacturing facility. What began as a small workshop has grown into a global enterprise serving farriers across 40+ countries.

Our founder, Mr. Ahmet Akbenli, recognized the need for high-quality, locally manufactured horseshoe nails that could compete with international standards. This vision drove us to invest in state-of-the-art forging technology and quality control systems.

Today, we remain the only Turkish company dedicated exclusively to horseshoe nail production, carrying forward a legacy of precision craftsmanship and manufacturing excellence.
